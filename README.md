# 🏨 Hotel Reservations Analysis — Revenue Leakage Study

> Analyzing 36,000+ hotel bookings to uncover $4.3M in revenue leakage  
> with actionable business recommendations using Power BI.

---

## 📌 Problem Statement
A hotel chain is losing significant revenue due to high cancellation rates and poor guest retention. This project identifies the root causes and provides data-driven recommendations to fix them.

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

| Problem | Finding | Impact |
|---|---|---|
| High Cancellations | 32.7% cancellation rate | $4.3M revenue lost |
| Lead Time Risk | 150+ day bookings cancel 80–90% of the time | Ghost bookings block rooms |
| Online Channel Risk | Biggest segment — but 36.5% cancellation rate | High volume ≠ high reliability |
| Zero Loyalty | 97% of guests never return | No stable repeat revenue |
| Flat Pricing | ADR stagnant in 2018 peak season | $2.5M opportunity missed |

---

## ✅ Recommendations

- **Stricter Deposits** — Mandatory non-refundable deposits for bookings 4+ months out
- **Target Corporate** — Prioritize Corporate/Aviation segments (cancel 3x less than Online)
- **Launch Loyalty Program** — A simple "Return Guest" offer to grow repeat business
- **Dynamic Pricing** — Hike rates during Autumn peak season
